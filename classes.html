Our Classes

Vinyasa Yoga

A dynamic, flowing yoga style that connects movement with breath for a powerful mind-body workout.

Power Yoga

A high-energy yoga practice focused on building strength, endurance, and flexibility.

Gentle Yoga

Perfect for all levels, this calming class encourages deep stretching and stress relief.

Mat Pilates

A core-focused class using bodyweight exercises to improve posture, stability, and tone.

Pilates Sculpt

Combines traditional Pilates with strength training techniques to shape and strengthen your body.

Gentle Core Pilates

Low-impact, beginner-friendly movements designed to activate your deep core and build strength gradually.

Kickboxing

An empowering cardio workout combining punches, kicks, and intervals to build power and endurance.

Cardio Kickboxing

A fast-paced fusion of aerobic exercise and kickboxing techniques to burn calories and boost stamina.

Combat Conditioning

An intense, full-body circuit class that builds functional strength and mental grit.
